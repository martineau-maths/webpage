Directed Diffusion-Limited Aggregation

2017

What is it about?

We introduce a directed version of DLA, define its dynamics in infinite volume and prove inequalities controlling the geometry of the growing cluster and the propagation of information along the dynamics. These results are thought of as very first steps towards an asymptotic shape theorem for Directed DLA.
