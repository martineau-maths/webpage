Locally infinite graphs and symmetries

2017

What is it about?

Because of compactness, locally finite graphs enjoy nice regularity properties. It is easy to find counterexamples to these regularity properties when the assumption of local finiteness is dropped. Do these properties still hold if we drop local finiteness but assume transitivity? This is precisely what is investigated in this paper.

The Graduate Journal of Mathematics, 2(2):42-50, 2017
